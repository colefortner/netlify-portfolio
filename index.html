Featured Project

Dogfriendly

Dogfriendly was my capstone project for All Aboard Ruby on Rails Bootcamp (March 2021 - April 2021 cohort)

A few years ago, I spent some time dog sitting my sister's dogs in St. Petersburg, Florida. While I was there, I discovered the most amazing culture of dog owners and dog friendly businesses I have ever seen. No matter where you went, there were dogs, and they were welcomed in bars, restaurants, beaches, just all over.

Being a dog owner from a city (St. Louis), that did not have a lot of dog friendly businesses, I thought it would be cool to create an app that could help owners find those establishments and perhaps foster a community.

This app makes it easier for owners to find those businesses/parks, and rate them for their dog friendliness/amenitites. The app also includes a social feature that allows users to connect with friends and check into businesses. Checking into a bar, restuarant, dog park, etc. will alert a user's friends to let them know where they can go to catch up with them and their dogs.
